== Description ==

Welcome to Spotless Cleaning, the ultimate free WordPress theme designed specifically for cleaning services and related businesses. With a sleek, modern design and a focus on user-friendliness, Spotless Cleaning offers a professional online presence that effortlessly showcases your services. This theme is fully responsive, ensuring that your website looks impeccable on any device, from desktops to smartphones. Customizable features allow you to tailor the theme to reflect your brand's unique identity, while pre-designed templates make setup quick and easy. Whether you're offering residential cleaning, commercial services, or specialized cleaning solutions, Spotless Cleaning provides a polished and engaging platform to attract and retain customers. Elevate your online presence with Spotless Cleaning and watch your business shine!

== Installation ==

Download the theme zip file from your account on our website.
Go to Appearance > Themes in your WordPress dashboard.
Click the "Add New" button at the top of the screen, then click "Upload Theme".
Choose the theme zip file and click "Install Now".
Once the theme is installed, click the "Activate" button to activate it.
